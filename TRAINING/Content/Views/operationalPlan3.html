KPI 2.2.3:

Number of municipalities for which spatial development frameworks are developed or reviewed

Definition

MISA technical consultants or appointed service providers assist municipalities to develop or review and update Spatial Development Frameworks (SDFs), Land Use Management Systems (LUMS), which include the following: procedures for municipal planning tribunals, municipal planning by-laws, land audits, land use schemes inclusive of register land use and zoning plans.

Measure

Quantitative (count of municipalities for which Spatial Development Frameworks, Land Use Management Schemes and Land Audits are developed or reviewed
