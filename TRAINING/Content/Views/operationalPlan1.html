KPI 2.2.1:

Number of municipalities with which annual technical support plans are signed and reported against quarterly

Definition

MISA technical consultants or MISA appointed service providers assist municipalities to put in place or review sector plans for municipal services such as roads, storm water, water and sanitation, energy, human settlements, LED, revenue enhancement plans, environmental management plans, disaster management plans and waste management plans

Measure

Quantitative (count of municipalities for which municipal infrastructure master plans are developed or reviewed)
