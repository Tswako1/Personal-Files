KPI 2.2.1:

Number of municipalities with which annual technical support plans are signed and reported against quarterly

Definition

MISA technical consultants provide advice and other forms of technical support to municipalities to assist them to plan and/ or deliver and / or maintain municipal infrastructure, i.e., roads, storm water, water, waste water, sanitation, energy, human settlement and waste management

Measure

Quantitative (Count of number of municipalities with annual technical support plans are signed and reported against)
