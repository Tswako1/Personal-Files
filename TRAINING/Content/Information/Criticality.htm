A Measurement of the Consequence of Failure and therefore an indicator of the "criticality" of the assets.

This basic approach is where the impacts of asset failure are contemplated using a simple rating approach.

The potential impacts are aggregated, resulting in the allocation of a criticality grading on a simple 3 point scale "Critical", "Important" and "Non-critical".

Area of Impact Measure Rating
