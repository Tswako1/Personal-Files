A consequence rating allocated to each risk event.

Consequence Rating Qualitative Description
Insignificant

Is readily absorbed under normal operating conditions

Minor

Can be managed under normal operating conditions

Moderate

Can be managed but requires additional resources and management effort

Major

Will have a prolonged impact and extensive consequences

Catastrophic

Irreversible and extensive impacts, or significantly undermining key business objectives

Source: GUIDELINES FOR INFRASTRUCTURE ASSET MANAGEMENT IN LOCAL GOVERNMENT 2006 – 2009 (DPLG)
